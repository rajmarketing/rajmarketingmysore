Velcro System

Product Description

The Velcro System is one of the simplest and most convenient mosquito net solutions. It's ideal for residential windows where easy installation and removal are key. The mesh is attached to the window frame using strong Velcro strips, allowing users to peel off and reattach the mesh effortlessly whenever required.

Specifications

• High-quality fiber mesh (available in black/grey)
• Strong industrial-grade Velcro tape
• Easy to cut and fit to any window size
• Invisible look from a distance
• Light-weight and flexible

Durability

The Velcro System typically lasts 2–3 years depending on usage and cleaning. The Velcro’s adhesive and grip are long-lasting under normal indoor conditions.

Maintenance

• Easy to clean — just peel off and wash the mesh
• Vacuum or rinse mesh with mild soap water
• Re-stick Velcro after cleaning using hand pressure
• Replace Velcro tape only if it loses stickiness over time
